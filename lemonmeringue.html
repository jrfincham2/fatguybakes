Perfect Lemon Meringue Pie

Ingredients

Pastry:
180g Plain Flour
50g Caster Sugar
1/4 tsp Salt
115g Cold Unsalted Butter, cubed
1 Large Egg

Filling:
250ml Fresh Lemon Juice
Zest of all lemons used
200g Butter
200g Caster Sugar
3 tbsp Cornflour
6 Egg Yolks
3 Whole Eggs

Meringue:
6 Egg Whites, room temp
300g Caster Sugar
3 tbsp Cornflour

Method

Pastry:
Pulse the ingredients in a food processor until they reach a bread crumb consistency.
Add cold water a little at a time until the pastry begins the come together.
Tip out the pastry onto a sheet of clingfilm, wrap and press together.
Gently press into a disk shape and chill, at least an hour, preferably overnight.
When ready to bake, roll out into a tin and blind bake at 180C

Filling:
Whisk sugar, zest and cornflour into the juice over a low heat, whisking constantly.
When it begins to thicken remove from heat add the butter and stir in until melted.
Beat the eggs and temper with some of the warm curd. Add to the pan and gently heat again.
Keep stirring or whisking until the eggs cook and the curd begins to go very thick.
Pour into cooked pastry case and allow to cool fully before adding the meringue.

Meringue:
Whisk the egg white until they reach soft peaks.
Mix the sugar and cornflour then add to the eggs.
Go slowly, about a tablespoon at a time to make sure the sugar gets fully incorporated into the meringue.
Top the cooled tart, try and get it right to the edges to properly seal over the pastry
Bake at 140c for 1hr - 1 1/2 hours until there's a thick crust on the meringue. Keep going if you like it darker
