Ingredients

Pastry:
360g Plain Flour
100g Caster Sugar
1/2 tsp Salt
230g Cold Unsalted Butter, cubed
2 Egg Yolks

Filling:
750ml Whole Milk
250ml Double Cream
250g Caster Sugar
3-4 tbsp Vanilla Extract/Paste
90g Cornflour
4 Egg Yolks
1 Whole Egg

Method

Pastry:
Pulse the ingredients in a food processor until they reach a bread crumb consistency.
Add cold water a little at a time until the pastry begins the come together.
Tip out the pastry onto a sheet of clingfilm, wrap and press together.
Gently press into a disk shape and chill, at least an hour, preferably overnight.
When ready to start roll out and line a deep flan tin. Chill again until custard is ready.

Filling:
Whisk together the cream and cornflour, adding in the eggs one at a time.
Whisk together the milk, vanilla and sugar and gradually bring to the boil, stirring constantly.
When the milk is boiling remove from the heat and temper into the cream mixture.
When fuly tempered, add back into the pan and heat back up being careful to scrape the side and bottom to prevent burning.
Once the custard has thickened pour into the pastry and bake in a preheated 200c oven for about an hour.
Once the top is thoroughly blackened remove from oven and cool for several hours or overnight to allow the custard to set
