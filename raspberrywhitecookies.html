Thick Raspberry & White Chocolate Cookies

Ingredients

225g White Bread Flour
150g Unsalted Butter
200g Light Brown Soft Sugar
1 Whole Egg
300g Chopped White Chocolate
1 tsp Vanilla Extract
1/2 tsp Salt
1/2 tsp Baking Powder
1/2 tsp Baking Soda
2 tbsp Cornflour
Pinch of Fresh Nutmeg

Method

Melt 50g butter in a pan and heat until solids separate and brown, then leave to cool.
Cream together the remaining butter, extract and sugar until fluffy. Mix in the egg, if making larger batch add one at a time.
Carefully mix in the remaining dry ingredients, being careful not to overmix.
Lastly, mix in the chocolate.
Shape into 100-120g dough balls and chill in the fridge for at least an hour. Cook in an oven preheated to 210c for 12 minutes.
Allow to cool for at least ten minutes.
